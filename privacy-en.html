Privacy Policy

Account & Subscription Shortcuts · Last updated: March 2026

1. Summary

Account & Subscription Shortcuts does not collect or transmit personal data to the developer’s servers. It only stores local preferences in your browser and opens official websites in a new tab.

2. Data the extension uses

The extension uses Chrome’s storage permission to save, only on your device:

  • Interface language (English or Spanish)
  • Your own shortcuts (optional): labels, section category, and URLs you add—e.g. your bank login page. This data never leaves your browser for the developer’s use.

It uses the windows permission only to open or focus the extension’s own shortcut panel window (a movable, resizable window). It does not read your browsing history or the contents of other windows for tracking.

This data stays on your device only. It is not synced to developer-operated servers.

3. How it works

Clicking the toolbar icon opens the shortcut list in a separate window you can move, resize, and close. When you click a shortcut, the matching web page opens in a new browser tab in the background; the shortcut window is brought back to the front so you can keep using it. The new tab stays in the browser until you select it. Sign-in and any information you enter on third-party sites happen only between you and that site. The extension does not read your passwords or page content.
