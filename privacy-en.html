Privacy Policy

Account & Subscription Shortcuts · Last updated: March 2026

1. Summary

Account & Subscription Shortcuts does not collect or transmit personal data to the developer’s servers. It only stores local preferences in your browser and opens official websites in a new tab.

2. Data the extension uses

The extension uses Chrome’s storage permission solely to save:

  • Interface language (English or Spanish)

This data stays on your device only. It is not synced to developer-operated servers.

3. How it works

When you click a shortcut, the matching web page opens (for example, an account or subscription management page). Sign-in and any information you enter happen only between you and that site. The extension does not read your passwords or page content.

4. Links

Destinations are public third-party URLs and may change; see the project source for the list shipped with each version.
